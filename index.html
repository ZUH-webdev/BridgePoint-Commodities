WHO WE ARE

Origin-direct commodity trade from West Africa

BridgePoint Commodities is a specialist trading firm connecting industrial buyers in North America and global markets directly to commodity supply in West Africa. We manage the full transaction — sourcing, quality control, documentation, logistics, and financing — so buyers get reliable execution without intermediary layers or margin leakage.

OUR EDGE

We hold established relationships with licensed producers and processors in Côte d'Ivoire built over more than a decade. Our team combines in-country supply access with North American commercial and capital markets expertise — a combination that gives buyers confidence from first conversation to final delivery.
